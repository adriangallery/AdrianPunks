TigerPunks · Provenance

Prove the 10,000 set is exactly what was minted — recomputed in your browser, checked against the chain. No trust required.

The on-chain PROVENANCE is sha256( masterSeed · row₁ · row₂ · … ) over every token's 10-byte trait row. This page rebuilds those rows from the published config + manifest, hashes them, and compares to TigerPunks.PROVENANCE() read live from Ethereum. A match means no trait was swapped after the fact.

or it auto-loads tigerpunks-config.json from this site if present

Reads PROVENANCE() (selector 0x9f86dd1b) via eth_call. Leave blank to just compute the hash.
